Overview

Browse global development data with custom visualizations. This project is an open source alternative to the World Bank world development indicators website, which also offers visualization tools.

To see the official website and access original data, refer to the World Bank website.

Data

To allow easier access to the original dataset, I’ve copied it into a Parse database. I’m happy to give others access to that, or a raw copy of its contents, but please don’t rely on my APIs directly – they are liable to change without warning. Contact information is on my website.
